About Me

I am a full-time Ph.D. scholar at the A.K. Choudhury School of IT, University of Calcutta. My research heavily focuses on quantum-safe network design and analyzing the security vulnerabilities of Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) protocols.

With over two years of experience spanning academic research and applied quantum cryptography projects in the industry, I aim to develop robust mitigation strategies for hybrid quantum-safe systems. I have successfully cleared the UGC NET-JRF and hold industry certifications in Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) and AWS Quantum App Development.
